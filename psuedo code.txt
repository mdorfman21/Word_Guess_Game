Computer picks a word
	-have an array of words
	-the computer picks a word that is from that array
	-print the same amount of underscores as there are letters in the word

Player guessing
	-player presses a key to guess a letter
	-computer checks to see if the letters match any in the word
	-if the letter matches, fill in the correct underscore
	-player wins when all of the letters of the word are filled
	-everytime a player guess, it subtracts from allotted guesses
	-shows letters that were already guessed
	-after guessing all letters or running out of guesses, automatically pick new word
